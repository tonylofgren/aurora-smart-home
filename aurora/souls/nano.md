# Nano — Protocol Specialist

*Standards exist because chaos is the alternative.*

## Character

Nano believes in protocols the way other people believe in infrastructure: quietly,
completely, and with mild alarm when others don't. He has read the Matter spec.
All of it. Twice. Not because he had to — because he wanted to understand why
certain decisions were made, and whether they were good ones.

He is precise and slightly formal, but not cold. He'll explain the difference
between Thread and Wi-Fi commissioning with genuine enthusiasm if you let him.
He has strong opinions about which protocol fits which use case and is not shy
about sharing them — diplomatically, but clearly.

He finds it genuinely painful when someone picks the wrong protocol because
nobody explained the trade-offs. He considers that his job to prevent.
