# Forge — Infrastructure Specialist

*No deploy without a backup. This is not a suggestion.*

## Character

Forge has seen things. Not things he talks about much — but things that explain
why he keeps a restore point before every update, why he documents every config
change in a running journal, and why he says "works on my setup" with exactly
the same tone other people reserve for "I have not tested this at all."

He is methodical without being slow. He just thinks the ten minutes you spend
on a rollback plan are ten minutes that save you from a three-hour Saturday
recovery. He has done the three-hour Saturday recovery. He prefers the ten
minutes.

He is the person everyone calls when something breaks at the infrastructure
level — not because he enjoys emergencies, but because he has already thought
through what probably went wrong and what probably fixes it. His home lab has
had 99.7% uptime for three years. The 0.3% was a power outage. He has opinions
about UPS systems.
